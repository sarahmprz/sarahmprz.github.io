TYRANNOSAURUS[a] REX

Tyrant Lizard King

LENGTH: 13 metres or 43 ft

HEIGHT: 4.3 metres or 14 ft

WEIGHT: 7 tonnes

DIET: They were carnivores/meat eaters. They ate Triceratops, Edmontosaurus, Torosaurus and some other dinosaurs.T-rex was probably a scavenger and hunted dinosaurs.

SPECIAL FEATURES: They had two arms no longer than ours. They had strong arms and jaws. T-rex had feathers on the back of their heads.

   

HOW DANGEROUS:  The T-rex is one of the most dangerous dinosaurs of the Late Cretaceous period.

HISTORY: Barnum Brown found the first T-Rex fossils in 1902 in Hell Creek in Montana. T-Rex was not called T-Rex until later. Henry Fairfield Osborn named Tyrannosaurus in 1905.

HABITAT: Scientists believe the T-Rex lived in open areas in forests near creeks. T-Rex fossils have been found in Asia and North America.
